About Us

M&G Innovations is a student-led engineering group focused on designing, testing, and improving small-scale propulsion systems and experimental hardware. Founded in 2024, our mission is to explore hands-on engineering, promote innovation, and develop cost-efficient engineering solutions through teamwork and experimentation.

Current Projects

  • Low-Cost Gas-Fueled Rocket Engine: Designed and tested small-scale engines with self-cooling systems.
  • CAD Manufacturing Designs: Developing 3D-modeled components for accessible, easy manufacturing.
  • Experimental Ignition Systems: Testing safe ignition for amateur propulsion.
