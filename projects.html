Projects

We design propulsion systems, engine prototypes, custom ignition modules, testing rigs, and experimental engineering hardware. Each project is fully student-built with a focus on innovation and reliability.

Pulse Jet Engine

We design and build compact pulse jet engines, experimenting with fuel efficiency, thrust, and noise optimization. These engines allow us to study pulse combustion and explore lightweight propulsion technologies for model rockets and other experimental devices.

Rocket Nozzles

Our team designs and fabricates custom rocket nozzles using CAD software and precise manufacturing. By testing different nozzle shapes and sizes, we improve thrust, fuel efficiency, and overall rocket performance in a controlled, educational environment.

Rockets

From backyard models to more complex experimental rockets, we develop vehicles that demonstrate real-world principles of aerodynamics, propulsion, and structural design. Safety and accessibility are a priority in all our rocket projects.

Rocket Engines

We construct and test small-scale rocket engines to understand thrust, combustion efficiency, and material performance under high temperatures. Our experiments give hands-on insight into the engineering behind full-scale propulsion systems.

Cannons

To study pressure dynamics and projectile motion, we build and test small cannons for experimental purposes. These projects help us learn about safety, materials engineering, and controlled energy release in mechanical systems.
